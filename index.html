NIT Rourkela Model United Nation

Model United Nations (MUN) is an educational simulation of the United Nations where the participants represent themselves as delegates of various countries. The NITR International Model United Nations is organized by Cognizen, Politics and Economics club of NIT Rourkela, every year. It desires to unify the future policymakers of the nation to discuss and debate on contemporary events throughout the globe.
